Roles

Newly qualified social worker

Newly qualified social workers are social workers in their first 2 years of practice. They may get extra support through the assessed and supported year in employment (ASYE) programme.

  • Salary range: £24,000 to £28,000

Social worker

Child and family social workers assess risk, protect children, provide support to parents and establish relationships with all family members.

  • Salary range: £28,000 to £40,000
