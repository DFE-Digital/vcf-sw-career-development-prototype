Social Work Leadership Pathways programme

The Social Work Leadership Pathways programme helps child and family social work leaders in England develop their skills, knowledge and behaviours.

It includes 4 separate pathways, each aimed at a different level of social work leadership.

Pathway 1: practice supervisors

For senior or consultant social workers, assistant team managers or advanced practitioners.

Pathway 2: middle managers

For current and aspiring team managers, service managers or independent reviewing officers (IROs).

Pathway 3: heads of service

For current and aspiring heads of service or senior managers.

Pathway 4: practice leaders

For current and aspiring directors or assistant directors.
