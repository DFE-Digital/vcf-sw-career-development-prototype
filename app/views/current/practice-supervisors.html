Programmes for practice supervisors

Practice supervisors can develop their leadership skills through the Social Work Leadership Pathways programme for child and family social workers.

The programme includes 4 separate pathways, each aimed at a different level of social work leadership.

Pathway 1: practice supervisors

For practice supervisors or those in an equivalent role to develop excellent social work practice and leadership in others.

Pathway 2: middle managers

For experienced practice supervisors who want to develop into team managers.
