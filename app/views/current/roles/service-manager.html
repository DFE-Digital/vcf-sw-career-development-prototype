Service manager

Salary range
£28,000 to £45,000 depending on location, responsibilities and experience
Summary
Assist children and families in need by providing assessments, interventions and ongoing support to improve their well-being and safety
Other names
Social work practitioner, frontline social worker

What you'll do

As a social worker you’ll:

  • conduct assessments of children and families to identify needs and risks
  • develop care plans that address the needs of children and families and promote their well-being
  • provide emotional and practical support to children and families
  • keep up-to-date records of assessments, care plans and progress reports
  • have regular supervision sessions with managers
  • work with multi-agency partners such as health professionals, schools and community organisations
  • take part in training and development to enhance your skills and knowledge

Skills and knowledge

You'll need to:

  • identify and analyse complex social, emotional and environmental issues affecting children and families
  • develop and put in place evidence-based care review plans
  • communicate effectively and build positive relationships with colleagues, service users and multi-agency partners
  • manage multiple cases at the same time
  • deal with difficult cases and situations and have self-care strategies to maintain your physical and emotional well-being
  • engage in reflective practice to enhance your skills and knowledge
  • take on feedback to improve your practice

How to become one

To become a team manager you must have a degree in social work and be registered with Social Work England.

You should also have:

  • at least 5 years' experience as a registered child and family social worker
  • experience working with multi-agency partners such as healthcare providers, schools and police services
  • experience managing budgets and resources

Career paths and progression

With experience you could:

  • progress into an advanced leadership role such as service manager or head of service
