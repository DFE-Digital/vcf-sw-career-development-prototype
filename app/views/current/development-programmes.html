DfE funded development programmes for social workers

The programmes we fund develop the skills and knowledge of social workers at all levels to help improve outcomes for children and families.

Assessed and Supported Year in Employment for child and family services (ASYE)

Newly qualified social workers

For newly qualified social workers who want to know about the assessed and supported year in employment (ASYE).

Social Work Leadership Pathways programme for child and family social workers

Pathway 1: practice supervisors

For practice supervisors or those in an equivalent role to develop excellent social work practice and leadership in others.

Pathway 2: middle managers

For team managers to influence and support the delivery of excellent social work practice within and beyond their team.

Pathway 3: heads of service

For current and aspiring heads of service to lead outstanding provision of social care through multiple teams and partnerships.

Pathway 4: practice leaders

For experienced heads of service (or equivalent) to develop their leadership skills and progress into a practice leader role.
