Assesment and intake

Assessment and intake teams carry out initial assessments with children and families to identify risk and determine appropriate support.

Child protection and safeguarding

Child protection and safeguarding teams protect children from harm, determine appropriate support and intervene in cases of immediate risk.

Courts

Court teams support children involved in legal proceedings and represent their interests in court.

Fostering

Fostering teams place children in safe and stable foster homes and provide ongoing support to children and foster carers.

Adoption

Adoption teams place children in safe and stable adoptive homes and provide ongoing support to children and adopters.

Youth justice

Youth justice teams support children to reduce the risk of offending and promote their positive development.

Children in care

Children in care teams ensure the safety and well-being of children looked after by local authorities and work to provide stable homes for them.

Leaving care

Leaving care teams ensure the safety of care leavers and provide the support they need to lead independent and fulfilling lives.

Children with disabilities

Children with disabilities teams support the well-being, independence and rights of children with disabilities and their families.

Adolescents

Adolescents teams are generally responsible for providing social work support to young people and their families who are facing various challenges or issues during adolescence.

Asylum-seeking children

Asylum-seeking children teams provide specialised support and assistance to asylum-seeking and refugee families, particularly those with children.

Mental health

Mental health teams support the mental well-being of children and young people, promoting positive family relationships, and ensuring a safe and nurturing environment for their growth and development

International social work

The international social work team's primary focus is on families with complex international connections, including families with members residing in different countries and those with a history of migration or cross-border issues.
