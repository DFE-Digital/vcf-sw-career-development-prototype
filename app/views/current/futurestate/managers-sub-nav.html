Development programmes

We fund the Social Work Leadership Pathways programme for child and family social workers.

It's delivered by the Frontline charity and includes 4 separate pathways, each aimed at a different level of social work leadership.

Social work managers can do Pathways 2 and 3.

Pathway 2: middle manager

For team managers to support the delivery of excellent social work practice within and beyond their team.

Pathway 3: heads of service

For experienced team managers who want to become heads of service.

View all programmes

Roles

Practice development manager

Practice development managers lead the development of social work teams to promote and support evidence-based best practice.

  • Salary range: £40,000 to £55,000

Quality improvement manager

Quality improvement managers audit and analyse the performance of social workers, teams and processes to ensure the delivery of high-quality social care.

  • Salary range: £38,000 to £65,000

Child protection advisor

Child protection advisors provide specialist guidance, support and leadership to ensure the safety and wellbeing of children.

  • Salary range: £38,000 to £45,000

Team manager

Team managers lead, develop and support a group of social workers to assess and manage risk to children and families.

  • Salary range: £45,000 to £65,000

Independent reviewing officer

Independent reviewing officers oversee and review care plans to ensure local authorities are acting in the best interests of children.

  • Salary range: £42,000 to £57,000
