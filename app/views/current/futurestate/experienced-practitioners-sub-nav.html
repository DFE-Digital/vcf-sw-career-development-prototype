Development programmes

We fund the Social Work Leadership Pathways programme for child and family social workers.

It's delivered by the Frontline charity and includes 4 separate pathways, each aimed at a different level of social work leadership.

Experienced practitioners can do Pathways 1 and 2.

Pathway 1: practice supervisors

For practice supervisors or equivalent to develop excellent social work practice and leadership in others.

Pathway 2: middle managers

For experienced practitioners who want to become middle or team managers.

View all programmes

Roles

Senior practitioner

Senior practitioners manage complex caseloads, provide quality assurance and mentor and support less experienced social workers.

  • Salary range: £38,000 to £50,000

Practice supervisor

Supervisors oversee the practice and decision-making of child and family practitioners and work with them to develop their skills.

  • Salary range: £35,000 to £55,000
