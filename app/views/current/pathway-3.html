Middle and managers and heads of service

Pathway 3: heads of service

For
Current and aspiring heads of service or service managers
Duration
12 months
Time commitment
About 11 days (including 2 residential components)
Cost

Free (except for travel for the residential components)

Website
Frontline

Who it’s for

Pathway 3 is for:

  • in-role heads of service and service managers
  • experienced middle managers who want to develop into a head of service

What you’ll learn

Pathway 3 will help you grow as a strategic service leader, focusing on:

  • developing an ambitious and inspiring vision for your service
  • prioritising and meeting the competing demands of a service
  • collaborating with multi-agency partners
  • managing risk and change across a whole service
  • putting equity, diversity and inclusion at the heart of your strategy

What you’ll get

When you finish Pathway 3 you’ll get:

  • a certificate which can contribute towards your continuous professional development (CPD) hours
  • access to leadership networks, including becoming part of the Frontline Fellowship community of leaders

It'll help prepare:

  • current heads of service to access Pathway 4 for practice leaders
  • aspiring heads of service for promotion to head of service roles

How it’s delivered

Pathway 3 has a time commitment of about 11 days spread over 12 months.

In-person learning

Includes:

  • 2 residential components at the University of Warwick (2 full days each)
  • an observational day visit

Online learning

Includes:

  • workshops
  • one-to-one coaching
  • peer-led practice groups
  • self-directed study sessions

Online modules last 1.5 to 3 hours each and happen several times a month.

Funding

Pathway 3 is funded by the Department for Education.

However, travel costs for the residential component are the responsibility of your employer.

How to apply

Speak to your line manager to get support for you doing Pathway 3.

If you’re an aspiring head of service, you’ll also need to answer questions about your work experience as part of your application.

Apply for Pathway 3 for heads of service on the Frontline website.
