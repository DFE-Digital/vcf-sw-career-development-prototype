Who it’s for

The Upon: aspirant directors programme is for social work leaders who aim to become directors of children’s services.

You should have at least 3 years’ senior strategic leadership and management experience in local government children’s services or related sectors.

What you’ll learn

This programme will help you develop your leadership and management skills, technical knowledge and confidence. It covers themes including:

  • building alliances and partnerships
  • working in political and corporate contexts
  • engaging communities
  • driving innovation and change

What you’ll get

This programme is not accredited. However, there is optional accreditation available to participants after the first year by Oxford Brookes University.

You’ll get career advice and support and become part of a national peer network during the programme and beyond.

You’ll also have ongoing access to our learning resources including guides, videos and blogs from successful leaders.

Funding

The Upon: aspirant directors programme is funded by the Department for Education.

Accommodation and meals for the on-site days are included, but your employer will need to cover the travel costs.

There is also a fee for the optional accreditation at the end of the programme.
