Programmes for leaders

The programmes we fund for social work leaders include:

  • Upon: Inspiring Leaders, delivered by The Staff College, The Institute of Public Care, Skills for Care and GatenbySanderson
  • the Social Work Leadership Pathways programme, delivered by the Frontline charity

The Pathways programme includes 4 separate pathways, each aimed at a different level of social work leadership.

Social work leaders can do Pathway 4.

Upon: aspiring directors

For social work leaders who want to become directors of children's services in the next 3 years.

Upon: new directors

For new directors of children's services to develop their leadership, management and technical skills.

Pathway 4: practice leaders

For practice leaders to lead, champion and improve services across their local system.
