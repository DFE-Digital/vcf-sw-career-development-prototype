Career information by career stage

The programmes we fund develop the skills and knowledge of social workers to help improve outcomes for children and families.


Practitioners

Includes newly qualified social workers and social workers.

Experienced practitioners

Includes senior or consultant social workers, practice educators and practice supervisors.

Managers

Includes team managers, middle managers and independent reviewing officers (IROs).

Senior managers

Includes principal social workers, heads of service and service managers.

Leaders

Includes assistant directors and directors of children's services.
