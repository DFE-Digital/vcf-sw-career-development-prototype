Who it’s for

The Upon: new directors programme is for directors of children's services who are new to their role.

What you’ll learn

This programme will help you develop your leadership and management skills, technical knowledge and confidence. It covers themes including:

  • building alliances and partnerships
  • working in political and corporate contexts
  • engaging communities
  • driving innovation and change

What you’ll get

You’ll get career advice and support and become part of a national peer network during the programme and beyond.

You’ll also have ongoing access to our learning resources including guides, videos and blogs from successful leaders.

How it’s delivered

The programme runs for 12 months and combines in-person and online learning.

Content will be tailored around the needs of the cohort, using the skills and experience of participants and the expertise of facilitators and contributors.

Funding

The Upon: new directors programme is funded by the Department for Education.

Accommodation and meals for the on-site days are included, but your employer will need to cover the travel costs.

Next steps

There is no application process for the Upon: new directors programme.

We'll be in touch to enrol you in the programme as soon as you start in your new post.

Find out more about the Upon: new directors programme on the Upon website.
