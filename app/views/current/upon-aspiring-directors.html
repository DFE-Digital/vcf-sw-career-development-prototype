Senior managers and practice leaders

Upon: aspirant directors programme

For
Assistant directors, strategic social workers, principal social workers
Duration
7 months
Time commitment
8 days
Cost

Free except for travel for in-person modules

Delivered by
The Staff College, The Institute of Public Care, Skills for Care and GatenbySanderson

Who it’s for

Social work leaders who aspire to become directors of children’s services.

You should have at least 3 years’ senior strategic leadership and management experience in local government children’s services or related sectors.

What you’ll learn

This programme will help you develop your leadership and management skills, technical knowledge and confidence. It covers themes including:

  • building alliances and partnerships
  • working in political and corporate contexts
  • engaging communities
  • driving innovation and change

You’ll have open access to a repository including guides, videos and blogs from successful leaders.

What you’ll get

How it’s delivered

There are 8 days of in-person and online learning spread over 7 months.

The next programme starts in September 2023.

Funding

The Upon: aspirant directors programme is funded by the Department for Education.

Accommodation and meals for in-person modules are included, but your employer will need to cover travel costs to and from the venue.

There is an optional accreditation option at the end of the programme which there will be a charge for.

Next steps

Speak to your line manager to get support for you doing the Upon: aspirant directors programme.

If you’re an aspiring head of service, you’ll also need to answer questions about your work experience as part of your application.

Find out more about Upon: aspirant directors including how to apply on the Upon website.
