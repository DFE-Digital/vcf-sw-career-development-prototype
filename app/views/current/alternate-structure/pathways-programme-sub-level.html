Social Work Leadership Pathways Programme

xxx.

The programme includes 4 separate pathways, each aimed at a different level of social work leadership. Pathways 2, 3 and 4 are for middle managers and heads of service.

Pathway 1: practice supervisors

For practice supervisors or those in an equivalent role who want to develop their leadership skills.

Pathway 2: middle managers

For middle leaders to drive impact and support the delivery of excellent social work practice within and beyond their team.

Pathway 3: heads of service

For current and aspiring heads of service to lead outstanding provision of social care through multiple teams and partnerships.

Pathway 4: practice leaders

For experienced heads of service (or equivalent) to develop their leadership skills and progress into a practice leader role.
