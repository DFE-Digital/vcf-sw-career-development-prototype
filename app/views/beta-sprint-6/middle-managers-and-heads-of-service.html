Programmes for middle managers and heads of service

We fund the Social Work Leadership Pathways programme for child and family social workers.

It's delivered by the Frontline charity and includes 4 separate pathways, each aimed at a different level of social work leadership.

Middle managers can do Pathways 2 and 3. Heads of service can do Pathways 3 and 4.

Pathway 2: middle managers

For team managers to influence and support the delivery of excellent social work practice within and beyond their team.

Pathway 3: heads of service

For current and aspiring heads of service to lead outstanding provision of social care through multiple teams and partnerships.

Pathway 4: practice leaders

For experienced heads of service (or equivalent) to develop their leadership skills and progress into a practice leader role.
