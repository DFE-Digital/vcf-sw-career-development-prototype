Development programmes

We fund the Social Work Leadership Pathways programme for child and family social workers.

It's delivered by the Frontline charity and includes 4 separate pathways, each aimed at a different level of social work leadership.

Senior managers can do Pathways 3 and 4.

Pathway 3: heads of service

For heads of service to lead outstanding provision of social care through multiple teams and partnerships.

Pathway 4: practice leaders

For experienced senior managers who want to become practice leaders.

View all programmes

Roles

Principal social worker

Principal social workers manage complex cases, support social work teams and contribute to the strategic leadership of services.

  • Salary range: £50,000 to £65,000

Service manager

Service managers oversee the performance and quality of practice of multiple teams of social workers across a service area.

  • Salary range: £46,000 to £70,000

Head of service

Heads of service oversee the day-to-day operations of services, manage staff and work closely with other agencies to promote best practice in social work.

  • Salary range: £60,000 to £80,000
